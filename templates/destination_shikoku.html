Shikoku

Shikoku is a land of spiritual journeys and hidden gems, where the 88-temple pilgrimage, dramatic coastlines, and rural charm invite exploration.

Ritsurin Garden is a place to relax and enjoy nature, on top of being able to take a boat ride.

If you want to go hiking halfway up Mount Zozu you will see Kotohiragu.

A castle is always cool to see, so go check out Matsuyama Castle. Or you can go to the Kochi castle.

At Otsuka Museum of Art you see amazingly beautiful reproductions of famous art work.

Tokushima Prefectural Uzu-no-Michi Walkway offers views of whirlpools in the Naruto Straight, that can be seen through a glass-floor walkway.

Chichibugahama Beach will mesmerize you with its vivid reflective waters.

Love cars go to Shikoku Automobile Museum

Or if you love towels go to The Towel Museum

Explore Ryugado Cave, to see interesting rock formations.
