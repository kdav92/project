Hakone

Hakone is a tranquil escape into nature, where hot springs, breathtaking mountain views, and glimpses of Mount Fuji create the perfect retreat.

Ok so you got Hakone. This an easy one day trip if you are staying in Tokyo. So here is what you should do!

Go to the Open Air Museum. It a sculputre museum all based outside.

Once you are done with that, you need to take a bus or a cable car to the Hakone Ropeway. The ropeway will take you over an active volcano up to Owakudani. You will see the sulfuric fumes being released. This volcano is the source to the mineral rich onsen, and the reason for the black eggs. It is believed that one egg adds seven years to your life, and make sure it sticks, you have to purchase 3 or 4 at a time!

But Hakone has a lot more to explore, like the actual onsen! Check out the other things while you are there too! There are museums. The Osawara Castle You can also go to the Hakone Shrine.

If you want to take it easy check out Hakone Gardens, where the flowers change with every season. There is also a cafe that uses Hakone ingredients, with a Danish theme.

If you're not tired of gardens yet, got to one with a waterfall; Hein Waterfall. You'll be able to see golden carp and ducks while you enjoy the view of the cascading waterfall.
