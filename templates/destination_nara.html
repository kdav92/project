Nara

Nara is the cradle of Japanese civilization, where sacred deer roam freely among the country's oldest temples and historical treasures.

Of the most famous things about Nara is its deer in Nara Park. There you can feed the deer. Be careful though, some deer are more aggressive than others.

If you're in Nara for the culture, check out Todai-ji Daibutsuden (Great Buddha Hall), home to one of the largest indoor seated Buddha. Make sure to try your luck with fitting through the "Buddha Nostril". Said to grant a degree of enlightenment in the next life for anyone that can fit through it.

Other historical places to check out:

  • Kofuku-ji Temple
  • Heijo Palace
  • Kasuga Taisha Shrine

    • Nara is very close to Osaka and Kyoto. How about planning your trip around all these places to get the best out your trip!
