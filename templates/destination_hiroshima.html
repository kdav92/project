Hiroshima

Hiroshima is a city of resilience and peace, where history's lessons stand alongside a thriving metropolis dedicated to hope and renewal.

If you travel to Hiroshima, you will feel the immense engery that that place brings. Start by going to the Atomic Bomb Dome. There are other peace dedications around there that you can also check out.

Right next to the Atomic Bomb Dome is the Hiroshima Peace Memorial Museum

Another place to visit is the Hiroshima Castle.

A popular place to go is Itsukushima Shrine, to see the floating Torii Gate. You will have to take a ferry to Miyajima Island to see the shrine. Make sure you stay long enough to see the shrine at high tide and low tide. Which is easy enough if you hike Mount Misen!

If you're into cars go the Mazda Museum.
