Sapporo

Sapporo blends urban energy with natural beauty, offering world-class beer, winter wonderlands, and sprawling landscapes that change with the seasons.

The first thing that comes to mind when you think of Sapporo should be the snow festival. If you like beer...that might be first, but more on that later. The famous snow festival is held every year around the beginning of Februrary and is held for one week. You'll get to see massive snow sculptures, which are beautiful during the day or night.

If beer is your thing, head to the Sapporo Beer Museum, a whole museum dedicated to beer. Learn about the long history of Sapporo Beer.

Once you are done with that, make your way to the Koji Shopping district.

Check out these two museums:

  • Museum of Modern Art
  • Sapporo Science Center

Sapporo is also home to many ski resorts. So check out those places a well if you are traveling during winter.
