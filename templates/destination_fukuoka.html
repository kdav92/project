Fukuoka

Fukuoka is a coastal gem where ancient temples, cutting-edge architecture, and a legendary ramen scene make for a dynamic and welcoming city.

There is a teamLab Forest in Fukuoka you should check out. You can capture the animals in the forest using an app on your phone.

The Fukuoka Tower is great place to see the views of Fukuoka from the observation deck. There is also a place for dining.

Tochoji Temple is famous for its huge wooden Buddha statue and ancient carvings.

Canal City is a huge shopping mall with brand names and many dining options.

Kushida shrine is where the a huge festival is held every year.

Dazaifu Tenmangu is a popular shrine that is dedicated to the god of learning. It also has gardens of plum trees.

If you go to Nanzoin Temple you will see a 19th century Buddhist temple that has a 41 meter long reclining Buddha statue.

Sakurai Futamigaura Couple Stone are two rocks that are joined together by a rope, connecting a male and female god. It also offers a beautiful scenic sunset view.

Check out Fukuoka Asian Art Museum. It shows contemporary and historic artwork from different Asian countries.

Kawachi Fujien is a place to enjoy the the tunnels draped with wisteria flowers.

For some fun activities good for children and adults check out NOBOLT.

By 6:00pm you are probably hungry so go check out the food stall.
