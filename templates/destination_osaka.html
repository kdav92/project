Osaka

Osaka is Japan's kitchen and entertainment capital, where vibrant street food, towering skyscrapers, and a boisterous spirit create an atmosphere unlike any other.

Ok fist and foremost, if you go to Osaka you need to go to Universal Studios Japan, USJ. Depending on what time of the year you go, it will be more crowded than usual. However, during New Years there is a special fireworks display, but make sure to plan ahead and get tickets early if that is what you want to do.

Osaka is home to teamLab Botanical Garden, which is best to check out at night.

Head your way to Dontonbori street. There you can try Osaka famous takoyaki. Along with taking a picture with the famous running man poster, just don't forget to make the pose with him.

Very close to Osaka is Kobe, where you can get Kobe famous wagyu beef. Just a 25 minute train ride from Osaka station to Kobe station!

Also check out the Osaka castle, which is around 61,000 square meters (15 acres), and contains thirteen structures, that are recongnized as "important cultural assets" by the Japanese government.

Osaka is very close to Kyoto, Nara, and Kobe. How about planning your trip around all these places to get the best out your trip!
